Maggi

Maggi is a quick and tasty instant noodle that is easy to make and loved by all age groups.

Ingredients

  • packet of maggi
  • water
  • salt
  • oil
  • turmeric
  • masala
  • chopped vegetables like onion, capsicum, tomato

Instructions

  • Boil water in a pan (about 1.5 cups).
  • Add Maggi noodles to the boiling water.
  • Add the masala (spice) packet that comes with it.
  • Cook for 2 minutes while stirring.
  • Turn off the heat and serve hot
