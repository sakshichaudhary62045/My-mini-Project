Tourism in Bihar

Exploring the Beauty of Bihar

Famous Tourist Places

Bihar is known for its rich history and cultural heritage. Some of the most famous places to visit are Bodh Gaya, Patna Sahib, and Nalanda University.

Cultural Heritage

The state is also famous for festivals like Chhath Puja and historical sites that reflect its ancient glory.

"Bihar is not just a place, it's a journey through history and culture."

Famous food of Bihar!

  • Litti-Chokha is a traditional dish from Bihar.
  • Litti is a round wheat flour ball stuffed with sattu (roasted gram flour).
  • Chokha is made of mashed roasted vegetables like eggplant, tomato, and potato.
  • It is usually served with ghee or butter and is very tasty.
